# Contact 

<div style="text-align: justify;">
<p>This website brings together blog articles, learning resources, and community-driven projects related to the Tashlhiyt language. Its goal is to share knowledge, support learning, and encourage participation in initiatives connected to language documentation, technology, and cultural transmission. Feedback, suggestions, and collaboration ideas are welcome from readers, contributors, educators, and researchers. You can reach us by email, and we will do our best to respond.
